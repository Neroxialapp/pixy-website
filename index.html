Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2024

1. Introduction: Pixy AI Labs ("we") is committed to protecting the privacy of Pixy users. This policy outlines our data practices.

2. Data Collection: We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children. User-provided data (logs, routines) is stored securely using industry-standard encryption (e.g., Google Firebase) solely to provide app functionality.

3. AI Features: Data processed by our AI models for generating stories or insights is anonymized and not used to train public models.

4. Third Parties: We do not sell, trade, or rent user data to third parties. We may use trusted analytics providers (like Google Analytics for Firebase) to improve app performance.

5. Contact: For privacy concerns, please contact us at support@pixykid.com.
