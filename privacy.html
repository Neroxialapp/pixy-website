Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 14, 2026

Pixy is designed specifically for parents to track their baby's growth journey securely. We prioritize the absolute security and privacy of your family's data above all else.

1. Data Controller

Managed by the Pixy Development Team.
Contact point: support@pixykid.com

2. Data We Collect

  • Account information & Baby's profiles
  • Growth data (height, weight, etc.)
  • Activity logs (sleep, feeding, diapers)
  • Anonymized usage analytics

3. Third-Party Services

We utilize robust, industry-standard infrastructure to ensure security and intelligence:

  • Google Firebase: Secure database & auth.
  • Google Gemini AI: Smart insights engine.
  • RevenueCat: Subscription infrastructure.

4. Account Deletion

You have full control. Users can permanently delete their account and all associated data instantly via 'Settings > Delete Account' within the Pixy app.
